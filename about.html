Welcome to Venezuela

Land of Sun, Nature and Traditions




Venezuela, a wonderful place to visit thanks to its geographical position, the variety of landscapes, the richness of plant and wildlife, the artistic expressions and its privileged tropical climate. This particular climate allows visitors to enjoy of any region of the country, especially the beaches, throughout the whole year.




Venezuela is a country of extraordinary diversity and abundant natural beauty, wildlife rich rainforests, vibrant cities and idillic Caribean islands. It is a place of good food, music and traditions, and nice people always ready to welcome visitors. For more details please click here.
